# Launch Guide

## Objectives
- Prepare communication, support, and operational readiness for public release.
- Align telemetry and dashboards with success metrics.

## Required Deliverables
- Launch brief (positioning, ICP, messaging, pricing).
- Support + operations playbook (FAQ, escalation flow).
- Dashboard/readiness checklist showing gating metrics.

## Evidence Checklist
- Launch tracker referencing dependencies, owners, and due dates.
- Links to `knowledge_base/launch_readiness.md` artifacts.
- Approvals from commercial/legal stakeholders recorded in `decisions.md`.

## Capability Hooks
- **Software:** finalises telemetry, release automation, and dashboards.
- Collaborates with GTM teams (content, sales) if applicable.

## Related Knowledge
